Wanna hear a joke? (Jokes API)

Get your Pokémon/ISS username here! (PokéAPI and Where the ISS at?)

Don't go too crazy using this generator — it'll raise hosting costs for PokéAPI and could surpass the "Where the ISS at?" request limit!

This username generator combines the name (full or partial) of a Pokémon with numbers related to the location of the ISS (International Space Station).
