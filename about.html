About this LHD: Build project

LHD: Build is a week-long hackathon comprised of mini-projects and challenges, and one of many, many hackathons hosted by Major League Hacking (MLH). See the Local Hack Day (LHD) live site for more details.

My participation in LHD: Build 2022 is part of my journey to self-learn programming and computer science, and just to practice coding as much as possible in my (quite limited) free time.

At this microsite here, you'll find some of my submissions to the 2022 LHD: Build challenges, though the mini-projects that aren't or perhaps can't necessarily be presented on this site will be found in the same GitHub repository.
