What exactly is this page?

Hi there, and thanks for visiting my microsite! As you may be able to tell from the title of this particular mini-project, this was styled using the Tailwind CSS framework. I also incorporated some Flowbite components in here — the navbar and two cards (one at the top, another beside or above this text depending on your screen size) were customized versions of their pre-made Tailwind components, so check out their docs if you like what you see!

Here are my favorite things about this page (aside from the fact that I learned to use Tailwind for the first time!):

  • It's fully responsive (though a few small adjustments could be made)
  • You can switch between light and dark mode! The colors could maybe be modified for improved aesthetics, but I'm glad to have managed this in a relatively short time. (Click anywhere between the logo and the circular toggle button on the navbar)
  • This was my submission to Day 3 of MLH's week-long hackathon, LHD: Build!

There's much, much more you can do with Tailwind than what's shown here — whether you're building a static website, or using frameworks or libraries like React, Next, Gatsby and more, there's a way to add Tailwind to your project. Visit the Tailwind CSS site to get started!
